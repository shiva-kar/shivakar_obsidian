### 1.2 Showing Output
- The printf function is used for output
- Syntax: `printf("format string", variable1, variable2, ...);`
- Displaying Text: `printf("Hello, World!");`
- New Line: Use `\n` within the string
- Format Specifiers:
  - `%d` or `%i`: for integers
  - `%c`: for characters
  - `%f`: for decimal numbers
