# **RRB JE (IT) – Full Details for BCA Graduates**

## **1. Age Limit**

General: **18 – 33 years**  
OBC (NCL): **18 – 36 years**  
SC/ST: **18 – 38 years**

Age relaxation also applies for:

- Ex-servicemen
    
- PwBD
    
- Railway staff (if applicable)
    

---

# **2. Syllabus**

## **CBT 1 Syllabus**

**Subjects:**

- Mathematics
    
- General Intelligence & Reasoning
    
- General Awareness
    
- General Science
    

**Breakdown:**

- Math: Profit & Loss, Time & Work, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration, Simple/Compound Interest, Statistics
    
- Reasoning: Analogies, Coding-Decoding, Syllogism, Venn Diagrams, Puzzle types
    
- General Awareness: Current Affairs, Indian Polity, Economy, Geography, History
    
- General Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (10th level)
    

---

## **CBT 2 Syllabus**

**Subjects:**

- General Awareness
    
- Physics & Chemistry (10th/12th level)
    
- Basics of Computers & Applications
    
- Fundamentals of Information Technology
    
- Coding & Programming
    

**Detailed Breakdown:**

### **Basics of Computers & Applications**

- Computer architecture
    
- Input/Output devices
    
- Memory types
    
- OS basics
    
- Networking fundamentals
    
- Internet, Email
    
- MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint
    

### **Fundamentals of IT**

- Number system
    
- Data representation
    
- Boolean algebra
    
- Networking types (LAN/WAN etc.)
    
- DBMS basics
    
- Software Engineering basics
    
- SDLC
    
- Security basics (virus, firewall, encryption)
    

### **Coding & Programming**

(Usually from C/C++ basics)

- Variables, Data Types
    
- Loops, Conditions
    
- Functions
    
- Arrays, Strings
    
- OOP basics
    
- Algorithms basics

# **3. Best Books to Study**

## **CBT 1 Books**

- Quant: _Quantitative Aptitude – R.S. Aggarwal_
    
- Reasoning: _A Modern Approach to Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning – R.S. Aggarwal_
    
- General Awareness: _Lucent GK_ + Current Affairs (monthly)
    
- General Science: NCERT Class 8–10
    

## **CBT 2 Books (Important for JE-IT)**

- _Computer Awareness – Arihant_
    
- _Objective Computer Awareness – R. Pillai_
    
- _Networking: Forouzan_ (basics only)
    
- _Programming in C – E. Balagurusamy_
    
- _DBMS – Korth or Navathe_
    
- _Operating System – Galvin (selected chapters)_
    

These cover everything needed.

# **4. Salary**

**Initial in-hand salary:** approx **₹42,000 – ₹46,000** per month.

Breakup:

- Pay Level: 6
    
- Basic Pay: ₹35,400
    
- DA + HRA + TA included
    
- Strong job stability + promotions
